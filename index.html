Moneyversity


TEAM

Anna Yuan
Jamie Park

ROLE

UI/UX Design, Research & Data Analysis

DESCRIPTION

Did you know that nearly two-thirds of Americans can't pass a basic test on financial literacy? Amid the COVID-19 pandemic, the need for better financial literacy skills has never been so present. To help address this issue, we created Moneyversity, a game-based online learning platform that aims to help young adults improve their financial literacy skills and apply them to real-world scenarios. Over the course of the Fall 2020 semester, my team and I designed this platform concept from ideation to final prototype.

Skinfo


TEAM

Solo Project - Just for fun!

ROLE

UI/UX Research & Design

DESCRIPTION

Skinfo is a skincare-oriented habit tracking and information sharing platform that aims to help users make informed decisions about their skincare habits and purchases. Especially since the COVID-19 pandemic, interest in skincare has been on the rise. The problem is, skincare is highly individualized and multifaceted, and there is no one-product-fits-all. Skinfo takes a consistent, long-term data collection approach to generate the personalized information that skincare users want to know.
